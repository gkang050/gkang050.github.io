Bio

Hi! Welcome to my digital playground! I am Gaganjot and I go by my short name, Gagan. Hailing from the northern region of India, I now call the sunny San Francisco Bay Area my home. I am a technology enthusiast with a knack for turning ideas into reality.
Academically and professionally, my background has always been in computer science and software engineering. When the world imagines software engineering, they often see an image of individuals immersed in lines of code. However, I perceive it as a way to bring pure joy to the person on the other side of the screen.
In my current role as a software engineer at Sony Interactive Entertainment (Sony PlayStation), I like to craft products that resonate with human emotions and leave them with a positive impact. In addition, I recently have had the privilege to be a part of an executive leadership program at Stanford Graduate School of Business where I further solidified my skills to scale this idea of engineering joy across diverse teams.
When I am not infront of my screen, I love to enjoy the nature through hikes, hit the gym for a good workout, and paddle with my rowing team, gearing up for local dragon boat races.
